QA Service

Splendid Fashion offers quality control as a standalone service to a number of customers who have already sourced their factories but need a little help to ensure that the factory reaches a certain quality level. Our services include:

AQLs: Splendid Fashion has an independent QA team that conducts AQLs on behalf of its customers according to the customers' AQL levels. Customers receive AQL decisions within 4 hours and the AQL report within 24 hours

Inline QA Checks : we place a full-time Quality Control manager in the factory to look after our client’s production. The QA’s role is to monitor the quality of the production from fabrics through to finishing, providing technical assistance to the factory where required and reporting back to the customer.

Pilot Run: the first production off the line, often known as the pilot run is the most important as it sets the tone for the full production run. In this capacity we are engaged to ensure that the pilot run is executed as per the customer’s required.

Sustainability

We are committed in preserving a robust and diverse natural environment where we operate. Our goal is to inspire individuals, catalyze positive change and empower goodness. As we continue to grow, we are proud to join the effort to sustain our natural environment. We are wholly committed to our responsibility to drive the sustainability of our operations, our client base, our stakeholders and our surrounding communities and environment. Our sustainability policy focuses on building capacity, resources and growth in the following core areas:

  1. People: Professional Development via education and healthcare initiative.

  2. Technology: Usage of technology in reducing every bit of energy possible.

  3. Quality: Usage of quality raw materials come up with savings at the end of the day.

  4. Environment: Splendid Fashion and all its divisions, operations and employees are all committed to achieving the highest possible standards of environmentally protective procedures.

We are continually evaluating and striving to improve our processes to reduce pollution and waste, conserve natural resources, and minimize potential negative environmental impacts of our activities and operations. As we believe in togetherness and we believe in tomorrow.
